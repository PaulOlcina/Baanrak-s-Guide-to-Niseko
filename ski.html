Annupuri

Relaxed and scenic ski area with wide groomed runs, great for beginners and intermediate skiers; quieter than Hirafu.

Moiwa

Smaller, peaceful resort known for excellent powder, fewer crowds, and a local, laid-back atmosphere.

Niseko Village

Large resort with long groomed runs, modern gondolas, and direct access from resort hotels.

Grand Hirafu

Most popular Niseko resort with lively village, extensive terrain, nightlife, and access to backcountry gates.

Hanazono

Modern resort area featuring wide open slopes, terrain parks, and family-friendly facilities.

Rusutsu

Large-scale ski resort near Niseko with varied terrain, tree skiing, amusement park, and excellent snow quality.
